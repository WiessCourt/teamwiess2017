Our New Fourth Floor RA!

May 16, 2017/RA Search Committee
Please join us in welcoming the newest member of the Wiess Family, Esther Fernández as our 4th floor RA! We are all so excited she will be joining us, contributing in her own way to the wonderfully dynamic community that we all share in. And she is equally if not more excited to be a part of Goldenrod Nation!
A bit about her: Esther is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Spanish, Portuguese, and Latin American Studies with a focus on early-modern Iberian literature and visual performance along with contemporary social and political issues in the Spanish-speaking world. This summer she will be conducting research in Madrid! Find more information about her on her faculty page. This is just the tip of the iceberg, and we encourage you to all to get to know her better over the next semester!
On behalf of the college we would also like to thank the all the members of the search committee, the Schaefers, and Tay for their service to Wiess throughout this process. You all did a fantastic job finding Wiess an RA who we are confident will do amazing things here.
