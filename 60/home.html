Wiess College 60th Anniversary Celebration


The year 2017 marks the 60th anniversary of the founding of Wiess College as one of the four original residential colleges at Rice. Six decades and one building later, Wiess is still the best. Make plans to join us at Homecoming Weekend 2017 to celebrate the past, present and future of Rice’s most envied and spirited college.
