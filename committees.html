Spirit Committee

Nasim Khalfe

The mission of the Spirit Committee is to promote Wiess spirit and spirit for Wiessmen. To foster care for all Wiessmen through initiatives that promote meaningful conversations, increase support for the activities Wiessmen participate in both inside and outside of Wiess, and encourage Wiessmen to follow their passions by creating an inclusive and loving environment.
